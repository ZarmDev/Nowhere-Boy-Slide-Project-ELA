Nowhere Boy Slide Project

ELA project on the book Nowhere Boy by Katherine Marsh.
    “there were no parents or grandparents sitting on the benches, no babies wailing from their strollers, no toddlers shouting up the slides or clambering up the mound. Ahmed instantly understood: people were afraid to be out.”
The theme is that even with differences, you can still make a living with others, Ahmed had a different religion, race and he still lived with Max and got to his father despite the rough situation.
